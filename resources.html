RESOURCES FOR REUSE

Reusing resources can help business and individuals save money while decreasing their ecological footprint. Reuse can also impact the community. Businesses can donate old materials and products to schools, creative art centers, or any number of reuse organizations. This benefits the community and avoids wasting good materials.
